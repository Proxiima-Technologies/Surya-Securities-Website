    Corporate Security Guards

    Corporate security Guards are employed by private companies to guard physical property and personnel from vandalism, theft, bodily harm, fire or illegal activity. They work in a variety of settings, from stores, office buildings, entertainment venues and banks to mobile security units.

    CMS (Central Monitoring System)

    As a security company we provide services to monitor 24/7 burglar, fire and residential alarm systems with the help of modern technology.

    Bouncers/Bodyguards

    Also known as close protection, refers to security and risk mitigation measures taken by us to ensure the safety of VIPs or other individuals who may be exposed to elevated personal risk because of their employment, high-profile status, net worth, affiliations or geographical location.We make sure all our EP’s goes through police background verification and enough physical and mental training before entering into field job.

    CCTV Supervisor

    Surya Services well equipped with latest technology and other electronic and surveillance equipment, offers expertise and targeted surveillance services to its valuable customers.Well Trained Supervisors are allotted this job.

    Manned Guarding

    Manned Guarding refers to a security guard guarding your premises, someone who is vetted, professional and trustworthy. The aim for a static security guard is to Guard the premises against unauthorised access or damage. Guard the property against destruction, damage and theft.

    Data Centre Security

    Data center security refers to the process of providing physical security safety and using virtual technologies to protect a data center from external and internal threats and attacks. Data centers provide services such as data storage, backup and recovery, data management and networking which involves highly critical information and it requires three layers of security protection.
