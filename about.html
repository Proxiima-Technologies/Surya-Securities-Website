About Us

About Us

We exclusively focus
on Top Security

Surya Services made an auspicious beginning in the year 1999, at a time when Pvt Security was in its most nascent stage. Its visionary Founder Shri Rakesh Tiwari could forsee its role & importance in years to come as a valuable adjunct to Police & Para Police Forces in meeting the internal security demands of the country. Since then Surya Services has been a trail blazer & a dominant player in its field.

Surya Services most distinctive feature is it being fully compliant to all the statutory obligations in full & within the laid down time schedule. It ensures SGs are paid wages in time ( not less than Minimum Govt Notified wages) & are covered by PF, Gratuity ,ESIC, Bonus, etc. Surya Services has a very sound infrastructure with a large fleet of 2 wheeler & 4 wheeler vehicles to provide full mobility to its operational team. To ensure full road worthiness of its vehicle fleet, SS has its own ‘state-of-the-art ’ Garage wherein vehicles from all the Branches are repaired & serviced.

As on date SS has Five fully operational Branches spread over Five States of India & has its presence in the areas of Maharashtra , Gujarat , Madhya Pradesh , Uttar Pradesh and Goa .It has a workforce of 2000+ fully trained, physically fit & mentally robust Security Guards(SGs).

Surya Services has operational staff of more than 100 officers/executives (from Area officers to Managers (ops) under Branch Heads) who maintain ‘round the clock’ vigil on the efficient & smooth functioning of the ‘ground level’ operations.
