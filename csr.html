Corporate Social Responsibility

We at Surya Services strongly believe and understand the responsibility of welfare to the society and employees associated with us. Different welfare schemes are in place in the company besides ESIC. The benefit of such schemes are extended to every employee of the company. We also understand the social responsibility equally and therefore Surya Services have been kind enough to donate various charitable institution for welfare of the society. This include girls education and education to the poorer of the society. Currently the Surya Services and it's promoter are contributing donation to various charitable institutions besides others social welfare.

Social Responsibilities

o Surya Services promoter has donated whole heartly to build a gorgeous Temple in Chakala Business Square which has become the landmark.

o Medical assistance and financial assistance for medical treatment to needy and financially weak people.

o Provided financial education to our Security guards to make them acheive financial freedom .

o Mr Rakesh Tiwari has arranged food distribution centre for needy people during COVID-19 outbreak.

o During lockdown period provided free food and full comfort to migrant workers.
